About Chalor

A New Standard in Institutional Capital Management

Chalor Capital Allocation & Institutional Group operates at the intersection of rigorous quantitative discipline and bespoke client mandate. Founded in 2019, the Group was established to bring the precision of a multi-family office to a broader spectrum of qualified investors — without sacrificing the institutional rigour that defines truly professional asset stewardship.

Our dual operating model integrates two distinct but complementary disciplines: systematic wealth management and strategic corporate identity architecture. The wealth management arm deploys a macro trend-following framework across global equity, fixed income, and ETF instruments, calibrated to deliver risk-adjusted returns within clearly defined volatility parameters. The corporate services arm applies the same precision to brand transformation and digital infrastructure for portfolio companies and mid-market enterprises.

"Access to institutional-grade capital allocation should not be contingent on the scale of one's initial mandate. We have structured our platform to ensure otherwise."

Operating across the DACH region, the Franco-Luxembourg regulatory axis, and the North American capital markets, Chalor provides its clients with a geographically diversified approach to allocation, custodianship, and liquidity management. Our custodian relationships span Clearstream and leading tier-1 European banks, ensuring settlement integrity and regulatory compliance across all mandates.

Geographic Coverage

Global Reach. Local Precision.

Three strategic axes form the geographic foundation of Chalor's operating model — each chosen for its distinct regulatory environment, capital market depth, and client base characteristics.

North America
NYSE · NASDAQ · SEC

Primary execution venue for equity mandates and ETF strategies. Deep-liquid, continuous markets with high-frequency rebalancing capability. Technology and growth sector allocation conducted under U.S. regulatory framework with full FATCA compliance infrastructure.

DACH & Continental Europe
Deutsche Börse · Xetra · BaFin

Core acquisition and advisory market. The German-speaking Mittelstand and broader KMU landscape represent Chalor's primary corporate client base for treasury management and brand architecture services. Frankfurt serves as the Group's operational centre of gravity within the EU.

Franco-Luxembourg Axis
Euronext · AMF · CSSF

Structural hub for fund domiciliation, custodianship, and cross-border mandate structuring. Luxembourg's UCITS framework and the AMF regulatory environment provide optimal conditions for the Group's private and institutional fund architecture mandates.
